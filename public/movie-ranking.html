The Best Anime According to Harshika

My top 3 Anime

Jojo's Bizzare Adventure

It is a classic anime known for its widely use in memes community. It's art style is one the most unique art styles among animes. A must watch for anyone looking for some adventure with a "you never know what's gonna happen next" flavour.

Kaguya Sama : Love is War

A very unique RomCom, featuring slow romance and mind games.

Kamisama Kiss

Classic supernatural romance
